This phase focuses on building the complete interactive dashboard suite using the KPI-Engineering dataset from Milestone 2.

In this phase four fully functional and interconnected dashboards were developed to enable dynamic exploration, comparison and coalition simulation of global military power across 140+ counturies.

**Input Dataset**

-Military_Data_With_KPIs.csv

-Contains:

-Engineered KPIs

-Military asset data

-Manpower Statistics

-Manpower indicators

-Economic indicators

**Quick stats and Nation Overview Dashboards**

**Objective** 
-Provide a global snapshot of military strength rankings and trends.

**Featured Implemented**

-Top 5 countries by Defense Budget

-Top 5 coutries by Military Assets

-Top 5 countries by Purchasing Power Parity

-Top 5 countries by air, Naval and Land Fleet

-Power index Rank KPI Card

**Nation Overview Dashboard**

 **Objective**
 -Provide detailed country-level military profile.

 **Features**

 -Country selector 

 -Power Index Rank KPI Card

 -Military Force Composition consisting of tanks, Aircraft and Naval

 -Manpower Composition consisting of Active Personnel, Paramilitary and Reserve Personnel

 -Assets Per Million Population

 **Compare Powers**

**Objective**
-Allow side-by-side comparison between any two countries.

**Features**

-Dual Country Selection

-Side-by-side KPI Comparison

-Rank Gap Analysis

-Asset Category Comparison

-Budget and Manpower Comparison

**Coalition Builder**

**Objective**
-Simulate alliance strength through multi-country aggregation.

**Features**

-Coalition VS Reference Country Comparison

-Structural Composition Analysis

-Automatic Recalculation of:

   - Total Assets

   - Personnel

   - Defense Budget

**Dashboard Integration**

-All dashboards are interconnected

-Filters are synchronized across the views.

-Navigation buttons implemented.

-Consistent formatting and visual theme applied

**Users can move smoothly between:**

Quick Stat - Nation Overview - Compare Powers - Coalition Builder
